Privacy Policy
Budget Wise AI

Last Updated: March 23, 2026  ·  Abdullah Salhab

We operates the Budget Wise AI mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Section 01

Information Collection & Use

Our app is designed to help you take full control of your personal budget. Here's exactly what data is involved and how it's handled:

💾
Financial Data All your transactions, budgets, and financial information are stored locally on your device using shared_preferences and local storage. We do not transmit this data to our servers.
🤖
AI Services When you use the AI Assistant, only the financial summary and questions you provide are sent to the Google Gemini API to generate insights. Data is processed per Google's AI privacy policies.
📢
Advertisements We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers to serve personalized ads.
Section 02

App Permissions

The app requests only the permissions it genuinely needs to function:

🌐 Internet — for ads & Gemini AI
🎙️ Microphone — optional voice commands
📁 Storage — CSV import & export
🔐 Biometric / PIN — local app lock
Section 03

Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. Since your financial data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's own security measures.

For sensitive items such as your PIN, we use flutter_secure_storage encryption to ensure they are protected even if the device is compromised.

Section 04

Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following trusted third-party providers:

Google Play Services AdMob Google Gemini AI
Section 05

Your Data Rights

You have full and complete control over your data at all times. You can delete all your transactions and budgets directly within the app's settings, or by clearing the app's cache and storage on your device — no request required.
