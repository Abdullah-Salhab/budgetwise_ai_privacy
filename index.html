Privacy Policy for Budget Wise AI Last Updated: March 23, 2026 Abdullah Salhab ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Budget Wise AI mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service. 1. Information Collection and Use Personal Data Our app is designed to help you track your budget. Financial Data: All your transactions, budgets, and financial information are stored locally on your device using shared_preferences and local storage. We do not transmit this data to our servers. AI Services: When you use the AI Assistant, only the financial summary and questions you provide are sent to the Google Gemini API to generate insights. This data is processed according to Google's AI privacy policies. Ads: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers to serve personalized ads. 2. Permissions The app requires the following permissions to function: Internet: To load ads and communicate with the Gemini AI engine. Microphone: Only used if you choose to use voice commands for adding transactions. Storage: Used for exporting and importing your data via CSV. Biometric/PIN: Used locally on your device to secure access to the app. 3. Data Security The security of your data is important to us. Since your financial data is stored locally, its security depends on your device's security. We use encryption (flutter_secure_storage) for sensitive items like your PIN. 4. Third-Party Services We use the following third-party services: Google Play Services AdMob Google Gemini AI 5. Your Data Rights You have full control over your data. You can delete all your transactions and budgets at any time within the app settings, or by clearing the app's cache/storage on your phone. 6.
